# Wach? Azul! Product Requirements

## Vision

Wach? Azul! is a magical adventure game that helps children of immigrant families learn Darja and Kabyle through story, family, culture, and play.

The product is not a vocabulary drill. It is a pride-building game where language feels connected to identity.

## North Star

We are not teaching words. We are helping a child feel proud of their language, family, culture, and identity.

## Target Player

- Young children, starting around age 4.
- Children who may not read confidently yet.
- Children in immigrant families who can benefit from joyful heritage-language exposure.

## Parent Promise

Parents should feel that Wach? Azul! is safe, respectful, local-first, culturally warm, and worth returning to daily.

## M1 Product Scope

M1 proves one magical local learning loop:

Splash -> Onboarding -> Child Profile -> Family Village -> Yemma -> Listen & Match -> XP -> Collectible -> Azul Celebration -> Return Tomorrow.

## M1 Must Include

- Local-only profile creation.
- Family Village as the first world.
- One meaningful family word: Yemma.
- One simple challenge a young child can understand.
- XP feedback.
- One collectible reward.
- A celebration moment with Azul.
- Persistence through local storage.

## M1 Must Not Include

- Supabase.
- Subscriptions.
- Parent dashboard.
- Multiple worlds.
- Large content library.
- Advanced progression systems.
- Network-dependent features.

## Success Signal

A child can complete the first loop with minimal help and feel curious, safe, proud, and excited to return tomorrow.

## Required Check

Before implementing a feature, ask:

- Does it support the North Star?
- Is it inside M1 scope?
- Does it create wonder?
- Can a 4-year-old understand what to do?
- Is it local-only for now?
- Does it avoid future features too early?
- Is it clean Flutter architecture?

Canonical extended requirements live in `docs/product/PRD.md`.
