# Wach? Azul! Constitution

Wach? Azul! exists to help children feel proud of their language, family, culture, and identity.

Every feature should serve warmth, cultural respect, clarity, and repeatable daily play.

## Non-Negotiables

- Build for Darja and Kabyle with care and humility.
- Keep the first experience small, joyful, and complete.
- Prefer offline-friendly local flows before networked systems.
- Keep child-facing screens calm, readable, and responsive.
- Use Riverpod, GoRouter, and feature-first Flutter architecture.
