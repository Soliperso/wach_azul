# Wach? Azul! --- Product Requirements Document

## Vision

Wach? Azul! is a magical adventure game helping children of immigrant
families learn their heritage language through exploration, stories,
family and play.

## North Star

We are not teaching words. We are helping children feel proud of their
language, family, culture and identity.

## MVP

-   Splash
-   Onboarding
-   Child Profile
-   Family Village
-   Azul Companion
-   One vocabulary lesson
-   One challenge
